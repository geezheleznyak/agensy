## Fault Lines

| Vault | Fault Line Statement |
|---|---|
| theoria | Is the universe's tendency toward increasing complexity an ontologically real tendency with normative significance — or an epistemic imposition (how we organize observations) or a normatively neutral mechanical byproduct? |
| bellum | Does the AI/drone/hypersonics/EW revolution create genuine discontinuity in the nature of war requiring new first principles — or do eternal principles maintain continuity and require only reapplication to new technology? |
| logos | N/A — expression vault; driving questions and fault lines are inherited from theoria and the accumulation vaults feeding it |
| politeia | Is political behavior primarily driven by material interests and structural constraints — or by ideas, identities, and beliefs that cannot be reduced to material conditions? |
| oeconomia | Are economic outcomes primarily determined by structural forces — debt cycles, technological paradigms, institutional path dependency, power distributions — or primarily shaped by ideas, narratives, and behavioral patterns that constitute the very structures they appear to reflect? |
| historia | Is the large-scale pattern of historical change primarily generated by structural forces (material conditions, geographic constraints, demographic pressures, institutional path dependency) that constrain actors into recognizable trajectories? Or is historical causation fundamentally shaped by contingent conjunctures (decisions, ideas, accidents) that no structural model could have predicted? |
